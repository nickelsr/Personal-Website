God rays

Ray Marching


I extended my pathtracing renderers capabilities to include volumetric particle rendering in addition to surface rendering. This was achieved by sampling volumetric areas on our rays path, preceding ray/surface intersection, for intersection with a light sources emission path, and combining that sample with the running Monte Carlo estimate. This creates a sunbeam-like effect (god ray).
